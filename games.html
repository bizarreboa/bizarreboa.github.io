Games

When life gives you lemons? Don't make lemonade. Make life take the lemons back! Get mad! I don't want your damn lemons, what am I supposed to do with these?

- Cave Johnson

huh idk

A huge hobby of mine is playing games. Mostly solo or co-op video games, but I love board games and card games as well. And when my undergraduate friends and I can all find the time, I enjoy playing dungeons and dragons.

Video games. It all started with minecraft, of course. I remember when the Minecraft website linked tutorial videos by paulsoaresjr, and I watched it all. In lieu of actually owning the game (my parents would never spend money on something that is such a pure waste of time), I learned to love watching let's plays on Youtube. I religiously watched every video in my subscriptions feed every single day. When I first got my credit card starting college, I bought Minecraft as my first purchase. My first love, truly.

I think Portal was one of the first games I finished. Hence the ode to the franchise in the quote up top.
