Research Interests

FRBs and their local environments.

Fast radio bursts (FRBs)are short-duration radio pulses from extragalactic sources that we don't yet understand. While the leading theory is a magnetar progenitor, the jury is not out!

One of the ways we can learn more about FRBs is through studying their host galaxies. Once we know where in the sky an FRB came from, we can associate it to a host galaxy and analyze the images and spectra of the host. This tells us what comprises the galaxy the FRB game from.
